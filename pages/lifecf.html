Life with CF

To patients effecting cystic fibrosis the symptoms and effects for each patient varies from each other. Hence the treatment procedures differ as well. Although there isn’t a specific treatment or cure for cystic fibrosis, nutrition’s, supplements and specific diets are used to minimize or cure other issues faced by cystic fibrosis patients. These issues include, digestive complications, lung infections and also resulting in some cases lung failures and a common issue in cystic fibrosis patients include loses of weight or fails to grow normally.

Cystic fibrosis patients needs to have medicines regularly as well as have doctor appointments and regular checkup as well as hours of physiotherapy. To minimize or balance other effects such as low digestive efficiency in cystic fibrosis patients extra 1000 calories is needed per day. Therefore to balance these issues cystic fibrosis patients have to consume more than 50 tablets a day.
